A work or several extracts of works made by the candidate and which are representative of his/ her specific practice. Only one file in digital format can be sent, 8 minutes maximum length, organised as the candidate wishes (for example: 3 video extracts, or a diaporama of photographs or a series of sound excerpts, or one or several views of video installations, etc.). Each extract of work should be preceded by a label indicating the nature and context of the work. This part of the application will be seen by two different juries.

Moreover, the jury will look at the visual material lasting 8 minutes maximum in the candidate’s portfolio (see artistic portfolio above). The candidate should note the importance of these documents, which are the only ones to be examined at all two stages of the selection process.

Il est important de noter que ce document vidéo est le seul qui sera vu par les membres du jury de l'entretien qui ne participent pas au jury de pré-sélection.
